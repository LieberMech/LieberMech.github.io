We are professionals

When it comes to indoor comfort and air quality, you and your family deserve only the best. Most importantly you also want a quality air conditioning and heating system that provides value as well as comfort. This is where Lieber Mechanical TLC Air Conditioning comes in. We're proud of over 60 years of high quality service in the Oklahoma City Metro area, providing residential and commercial customers alike with complete sales and installation of heating and air conditioning systems such as Carrier, Climate Master, Comfortmaker, American Standard, Rheem, LG, Daikin, Mitsubishi and more, plus if you suffer from allergies, Lieber Mechanical TLC offers excellent air quality systems at affordable prices.

Lieber Mechanical TLC services all makes and models and offers a "Planned Service Agreement" to keep your home system run efficiently and cost effectively. With Lieber Mechanical TLC's continued education through the "Refrigeration Service Engineers Society" you can be sure that the most up-to-date evaluation of your home unit will be made. TLC is a proud member of the Mustang Chamber of Commerce, Air Conditioning Contractors of America, International Ground Source Heat Pump Association, a Lead Safe Certified Firm and is family owned and operated. When it comes to indoor comfort and air quality, you and your family deserve TOTAL LIVING COMFORT!
